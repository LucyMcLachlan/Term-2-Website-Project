Skylanders: Swap Force

The Game

The magic of Skylands is replensished every hundred year by the four elementals who cause a giant magical volcanoe to explode. But after the skylanders the protectors of Skylands get thrown to earth by the explosion of the volcanoe they're given the power to swap their top and bottom halfs with other skylanders expanding their powers. Using these new powers the skylanders must stop Kaos our (small goblin like) main villain from turning the elementals evil with his evil magic and then get back to Skylands.

How to Play

Skylanders is a 3D platformer game where small figurines are used to unlock playable characters. On the Wii you move with the joystick remote attachment with a variety of other buttons being different attacks. Differnt characters have different specialties, types and move sets.
